About Us

We are developer and Students of Kalasalingam University with our project AgroTech

This web based application is a complete package for farmers who are not much experienced in farming, have a small land and are living in the vicious cycle of middleman and vendors as well as retailers who wants to buy food essentials from anywhere to find good quality and price. This application:

  • It gives complete information about what crop to grow, what fertilizers and pesticides to use depending upon land condition and weather conditions.
  • It provides a platform that connects farmers directly to consumers or retailers thus, eliminates middlemen.
  • It can be used by both farmers as well as retailers/consumers accordingly.
